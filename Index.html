IDEOLOGY

My take on achievements has changed quite a bit, especially in the last two or three years. What I believe now is that achievements are nothing more than certfication or more appropriately approval. Do you need an approval for honesty? or trust? or establishing skills? I know it sounds absurd, how is achievement related to values? Its not (directly atleast). But it all comes down to the self. Its something you realise is important for you and you are not doing it for someone else. Hence, if you know something, you do not need anyone documentation to prove it to yourself. The only approval you need is, youselves.
